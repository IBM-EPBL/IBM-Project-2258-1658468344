Loan prediction is a method of predicting the future value of a loan. It is based on historical data and various other factors such as interest rates, inflation rate, GDP growth etc. The idea behind this is that there are certain trends in the economy which will continue to exist in the future too. This helps us predict how much money we can get back from our loans when they come due.
